The technology

Worn once. Learns
always.

01
Apply to Skin

A soft, medical-grade adhesive patch — barely larger than a coin — sits comfortably on your upper back. It's waterproof, breathable, and invisible under clothing.

02
Continuous Sensing

Six embedded biosensors capture IMU data for posture, galvanic skin response for stress, ambient light for screen time, heart rate variability, temperature, and micro-movement patterns.

03
Intelligent Insight

Raw sensor data is processed by on-device AI and synced to the Align app. Patterns emerge over days. Personalized nudges arrive exactly when you need them.

Posture Intelligence

Real-time spinal alignment tracking using a 9-axis IMU. Detect forward head posture, slouching, and asymmetry with sub-degree precision.

IMU + AI

Stress Monitoring

Galvanic skin conductance and HRV analysis continuously measure autonomic nervous system state — flagging stress spikes before they become chronic.

GSR + HRV

Screen Time Exposure

Ambient light spectrum sensors detect blue light saturation and proximity patterns to screens — tracking digital exposure without any phone integration required.

Light Sensing

Mood Inference

A composite model of skin response, movement entropy, breathing rate, and circadian rhythm data infers emotional valence — grounding mood in physiology, not self-report.

ML Model

Activity & Recovery

Classify sedentary periods, active bursts, and recovery windows. Align integrates with your calendar to match physical state against your commitments.

Motion AI

Circadian Rhythm

Monitor your energy rhythms throughout the day. Align learns your chronotype and surfaces your peak focus, social, and rest windows with pinpoint precision.
