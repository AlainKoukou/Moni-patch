The technology

Worn once. Learns
always.

01
Apply to Skin

A soft, medical-grade adhesive patch — barely larger than a coin — sits comfortably on your upper back. It is waterproof, breathable, and invisible under clothing.

02
Continuous Sensing

Six embedded biosensors capture IMU data for posture, galvanic skin response for stress, ambient light for screen time, heart rate variability, temperature, and micro-movement patterns.

03
Intelligent Insight

Raw sensor data is processed by on-device AI and synced to the Align app. Patterns emerge over time, and personalized nudges arrive when they matter most.

What Align tracks

Six dimensions
of you.

Posture Intelligence

Real-time spinal alignment tracking using a 9-axis IMU. Detect forward head posture, slouching, and asymmetry with sub-degree precision.

IMU + AI

Stress Monitoring

Galvanic skin conductance and HRV analysis continuously measure autonomic nervous system state — flagging stress spikes before they become chronic.

GSR + HRV

Screen Time Exposure

Ambient light spectrum sensors detect blue light saturation and proximity patterns to screens — tracking digital exposure without requiring phone integration.

Light Sensing

Mood Inference

A composite model of skin response, movement entropy, breathing rate, and circadian rhythm data infers emotional valence — grounding mood in physiology, not self-report.

ML Model

Activity & Recovery

Classify sedentary periods, active bursts, and recovery windows. Align can contextualize physical state against routine and workload.

Motion AI

Circadian Rhythm

Monitor energy patterns throughout the day. Align learns chronotype and surfaces your peak focus, social, and rest windows with precision.

Temporal AI
